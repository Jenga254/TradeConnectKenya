Welcome to TradeConnect

TradeConnect is your go-to platform for hiring skilled, verified tradespeople across Kenya. Whether you’re looking for a reliable plumber, electrician, mason, or carpenter, we connect you with trusted professionals in your area—fast and hassle-free.

For clients, posting a job is simple and free. Just share your requirements and receive applications from qualified tradespeople. For professionals, it's a chance to showcase your skills, get hired, and grow your business.

No middlemen, no guesswork—just quality work, done right.
